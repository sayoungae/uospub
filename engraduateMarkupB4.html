Graduate School admission capacity increase

Master's: Including newly established Physics and Semiconductor Engineering, +54 = 255 capacity

Doctorate's: Newly established Control and Instrumentation Engineering, +5 = 74 capacity

Graduate School department establishment, merger, capacity adjustment

  • Master's program

    • - Including newly established Life Sciences, +6 = 261 capacity
    • - Electronic Engineering, Control and Instrumentation Engineering, Semiconductor Engineering → Merged into Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering

  • Doctorate's program

    • - Including newly established Korean History, +2 = 76 capacity
    • - Electronic Engineering, Control and Instrumentation Engineering → Merged into Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering

Jul 18, 2000

Graduate School capacity +11 (6 master's, 5 doctorate's)

  • Newly established Social Welfare master's program

  • Department name changes

    • - Electronic and Electrical Engineering → Electronic, Electrical and Computer Engineering
    • - Precision Mechanical Engineering → Mechanical and Information Engineering
    • - Computer Science and Statistics → Computer Science and Statistics

Jul 28, 2001

Graduate School capacity +26 (15 master's, 11 doctorate's)

Newly established departments

  • - Master's: Urban Sociology, Nanoscience and Technology
  • - Doctorate's: Department of Life Sciences
Oct 30, 2002

Graduate School capacity adjustment

  • Doctorate's program +10, Newly established Industry-University-Research Cooperative Program (Korea Institute of Science and Technology) +3
  • Master's program +18, Industry-University-Research Cooperative Program (Korea Institute of Science and Technology) newly established +4
  • Newly established departments: Geoinformatics (master's), Architecture (master's/doctorate's)

  • Industry-University-Research Cooperative Program (master's/doctorate's) newly established: Agreement with Korea Institute of Science and Technology

    ※ Recruitment unit name change: General Graduate School Materials Engineering → Advanced Materials Engineering
Sep 27, 2003

Graduate School capacity adjustment

Decrease by 5 (master's), Industrial Design major and Product Design major within Industrial Art department (abolished), Tax Studies (abolished)

  • - Recruitment unit name change: Industrial Art → Environmental Sculpture
  • - Major fields: Computer Science and Statistics → Separated into Computer Science major and Statistics major

2005 Graduate School Doctorate's program establishment (autonomous decision within total capacity)

  • Newly established departments: 2 Doctorate's programs, 9 students

    • - Department of Geoinformatics: 4 students
    • - Department of Social Welfare 5 students

  • Decrease: 2 master's programs, 9 students

    • - Department of Geoinformatics: -4 (8 → 4)
    • - Department of Social Welfare -5 (15 → 10)
Aug 29, 2005

2006 Academic Year Graduate School capacity adjustment

  • Capacity adjustment between degree programs

    • - Newly established: Interdepartmental Cooperative Doctorate's program 5 (Nanoengineering)
    • - Reduction: Doctorate's program -5 = 133 → 128 capacity

  • Department consolidation

    • - Business Administration + Accounting ⇒ Business Administration, International Trade + Economics ⇒ Economics
Sep 7, 2006

2007 Academic Year Graduate School capacity adjustment

  • Capacity adjustment between degree programs

    • - Increase: Interdepartmental Cooperative program +1 = 5 → 6 capacity
    • - Decrease: Doctorate's program -1 = 128 → 127 capacity
  • Newly established Interdepartmental Cooperative Disaster Science (doctorate's program)

  • Newly established departments: Master's program in 1 department (within the total capacity of the Department of Music)

  • Capacity increase: 53 students

    • - Doctorate's program +20 = 136 → 156 capacity

  • Department closure: Law Department for master's and doctorate's programs
  • Department establishment: Two doctorate's programs (Philosophy, Urban Sociology)

  • Capacity adjustment

    • - Master's program -5 = 387 → 382 capacity
    • - Doctorate's program +5 = 156 → 161 capacity
Oct 15, 2009

2010 academic year graduate school department establishment

Establishment of the master's and doctorate's programs in Urban Environmental Systems Engineering (within the total capacity)
Jul 15, 2010 Change of the name of Urban Environmental Systems Engineering to Energy and Environmental Systems Engineering
Nov 29, 2010

2011 Academic Year Graduate School department establishment and capacity increase

  • Separate establishment of Department of Statistics (Natural Sciences) and Computer Science Department (Engineering)
  • Establishment of master's program in Department of Sports Information (Arts & Sports)

  • Capacity increase

    • - Master's program +5 = 382 → 387 capacity
