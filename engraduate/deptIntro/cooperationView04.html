Educational objectives

Core objectives Strengthening our university's international competitiveness in international urban science research and nurturing international urban development experts
Detailed objectives
  • Providing a research, practical, and industry-linked network platform in the fields of international urban infrastructure and urban policy for sharing urban policies between countries and mutual development
  • Training international urban development and overseas construction experts equipped with theoretical foundations, professional knowledge, and practical capabilities to actively and effectively respond to international urban development and overseas construction.
  • Experts who possess professionalism and skills based on rich cultural knowledge and proper values through interdisciplinary education.
Research areas Smart mobility, Smart urban regeneration, Smart safety and disaster prevention, Smart energy and environment, Smart spatial information
