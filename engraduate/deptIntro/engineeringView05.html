Educational objectives

Core objectives The Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering aims to cultivate excellent technical personnel who can play creative and leading roles in the development of technology and academia in advanced fields of high-industrial society, such as electrical engineering, semiconductor engineering, control and measurement instruments, and automation. This includes conducting research on theory and application and accumulating technology and data to contribute to the academic and technological development of Korea's industries.
Research areas Electrical and electronic engineering, computer science

Majors (track)

Electrical and Electronic Engineering Cultivate in-depth mathematical, scientific, and engineering knowledge in electrical and electronic engineering, understand theories, and develop the ability to use necessary technologies and methods, as well as advanced engineering tools, to solve complex problems and produce leader-like researchers and technologists in society
Computer Science Cultivate in-depth mathematical, scientific, and engineering knowledge necessary for computer science, understand theories, and develop the ability to use necessary technologies and methods, as well as advanced engineering tools, to solve complex problems and produce leader-like researchers and technologists in society

Minimum publication requirements for doctoral dissertation

  • At least two papers as the first author or corresponding author in journals recognized by the National Research Foundation of Korea or higher
  • Or at least 1 paper published as first author in SCI(E) listed journals or higher
