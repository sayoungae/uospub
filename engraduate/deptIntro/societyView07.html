Educational objectives

Core objectives As it is necessary to cultivate practical talents who will lead sustainable development through multifaceted analysis and creative-innovative solutions to complex economic and social problems, we cultivate advanced future talents equipped with 'practical problem-solving competency', 'convergence and creativity competency', 'academic basic competency' and 'specialization competency'
Detailed objectives
  • 1. 'Practical problem-solving competency' that can lead the resolution of various economic problems
  • 2. 'Convergence creativity competency' to accommodate and apply complex and multifaceted changes in the era of the 4th industrial revolution
  • 3. Qualitative growth of 'academic basic competency' and 'specialization competency' as competitive future talents
  • 4. Cultivation of thesis writing ability: Acquisition of specialized knowledge in specific professional fields, activation of seminars, acquisition of thesis writing methods
