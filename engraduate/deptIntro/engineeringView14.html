Educational objectives

Core objectives The Department of Intelligent Semiconductor Engineering aims to cultivate excellent technical personnel who can play creative and leading roles in the development of technology and academia in the semiconductor material, device, process, circuit, and system fields, as well as conduct research on theory and application and accumulate technology and data to contribute to the academic and technological development of Korea's industries.
Detailed objectives
  • Providing diverse and specialized education aimed at developing semiconductor materials, devices, and processes
  • Providing diverse and specialized education aimed at designing semiconductor circuits and systems
Research areas Semiconductor material field, semiconductor device field, semiconductor process field, semiconductor circuit field, semiconductor system field, or fields related to semiconductor materials, devices, processes, circuits, and systems

Minimum publication requirements for doctoral dissertation

  • At least two papers as the first author or corresponding author in journals recognized by the National Research Foundation of Korea or higher or at least one paper in an SCI(E) indexed journal or higher
