Educational objectives

Core objectives The educational goal is to cultivate understanding and analytical skills for international issues and regional research professionals needed in the era of globalization and informatization.
Detailed objectives
  • 1. Cultivate regional experts suitable for the era of globalization and regionalization: Cultivate experts with practical experience and in-depth knowledge of the relevant regions
  • 2. Cultivate regional researchers: Cultivate sustainable research capabilities (strengthen research methodology and connection with relevant regions)
  • 3. Cultivation of international relations researchers: Strengthening the qualities needed as international trade and exchange experts
Research areas East Asian regional studies, American and European regional studies, International relations studies

Majors (track)

East Asian Regional Studies Study of East Asian international relations and relations with the Korean Peninsula based on East Asian politics/economics
American and European Regional Studies Study of American and European regional politics/economics and their impact on international society, international relations phenomena
International Relations Studies International relations research responding to the era of globalization and regionalization
