Educational objectives

Core objectives The core objective is to cultivate professional sculptors who can play a future-oriented leadership role in the field of visual arts based on a creative and challenging spirit Through this, we aim to create a new type of culture that can expand expertise and creativity in the field of fine arts, as well as communication with general society.
Detailed objectives

  • 1. Strengthening academic capabilities

    • 1-1 Further deepen and expand knowledge of contemporary art criticism and cultural context.

  • 2. Enhancing artistic self-expression

    • 2-1 Research contemporary sculpture trends and analyze artistic concepts based on various perspectives
    • 2-2 Expand artistic creativity and build the ability to convey social messages through exploration of various cultural theories and social issues.
    • 2-3 Analyze forms using various materials and create original expression techniques and sculptural works.
Research areas Fine art sculpture and applied fields (art criticism, curator, designer)

Department amenities and publicity

  • Educational Environment: Possession of various experimental practice equipment and operation of excellent facilities
  • Metal Sculpture Studio: Plasma cutter, metal cutter, hydraulic shear, bench grinder, belt sander, TIG welder, MIG welder, oxygen-acetylene welder, spot welder, drill press, hoist crane
  • Wood Sculpture Studio: Table saw, band saw, automatic planer, belt sander, angle cutter, plunge saw, dust collection system
  • Computer Room: About 30 high-performance computers, beam projector, multimedia A/V system, plotter for actual printing
  • Audiovisual Room: Beam projector, multimedia A/V system
  • Sculpture Studio: Sculpture stands, clay kneader, model pedestals
  • Machine Workshop: Acrylic heat wire bender, tabletop rotary saw
  • Painting Room: Painting of various sculpture materials
  • Outdoor Machine Workshop: High-performance 3D printer, CNC machine
  • Other: Electric kiln
