Educational objectives

Core objectives The basic philosophy is to realize human happiness through improving the level of sports welfare The educational goal is to research various phenomena related to sports science and establish new theories to contribute to the realization of sports welfare and the improvement of national health and quality of life.
Detailed objectives

  • 1. Cultivating competent academic successors in sports science: The goal is to develop superior academic fields such as sports health and sports industry, and to cultivate excellent human resources needed in related fields through training experts.

  • 2. Fostering sports science practical experts based on specialization

    • 2-1 Based on the characteristics of an urban science specialized university, we cultivate professional personnel related to sports health and elderly health for relevant institutions such as hospitals, health centers, elderly care and treatment facilities through education of sports health medicine experts in preparation for health problems in an aging society.
    • 2-2 For the sports industry market, which is emerging as a high value-added and convergence industry in the 21st century, we cultivate professional personnel with scientific thinking abilities and specialized knowledge to lead private companies and professional sports derivative industries, public institutions and sports organizations, and IT media information markets and leisure markets.
    • 2-3. We cultivate professional personnel who can make academic and practical contributions to the promotion of sports welfare and improvement of people's quality of life by focusing on theory development and field research for the science, informatization, and popularization of sports.
Research areas Sports psychology, Sports industry, Sports medicine, Elderly health, Exercise physiology, Leisure and recreation, Biomechanics, Adapted physical education, Exercise nutrition biochemistry, Sports neuroscience, Sports sociology

Minimum publication requirements for doctoral dissertation

  • At least 2 papers published as first author in journals listed by the National Research Foundation of Korea (KCI) or higher

  • Or at least 1 paper published as first author in SCI(E) listed journals or higher

    ※ For Scopus, papers written in Korean correspond to KCI level / papers written in English correspond to SCI(E) level
