Educational objectives

Core objectives The Department of Convergent Applied Chemistry aims to cultivate chemical experts who can solve various future societal problems related to health, environment, and energy by covering fields such as advanced new materials, new energy industries, environment, biohealth, and innovative new drugs.
Detailed objectives
  • 1. Cultivate internationally competitive chemical experts
  • 2. Secure employment expertise through industry-academia-research collaboration programs
  • 3. Cultivate interdisciplinary convergence chemistry experts suitable for solving future societal problems
  • 4. Activate integrated programs from bachelor's to master's and from master's to doctorate's
Research areas Computational chemistry, energy chemistry, environmental chemistry, nanochemistry, analytical chemistry, etc.
