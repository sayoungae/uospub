Educational objectives

Core objectives The Chinese language and culture master's program aims to cultivate excellent talents who will contribute to deepening our society's knowledge and perspective on China by professionally researching topics related to Chinese language and culture.
Graduates who have grown into professional researchers through systematic and intensive training will be able to immediately advance to higher educational institutions to continue in-depth academic research, or enter media companies, corporations, etc. to demonstrate their capabilities as experts.
Detailed objectives
  • 1. Cultivate basic research skills as a researcher of Chinese language and culture.
  • 2. Understand the system of knowledge related to Chinese language and culture, existing research achievements, and research methodologies.
  • 3. Develop research capabilities to conduct original research on Chinese language and culture and produce new knowledge.
Research areas Fields related to Chinese language, fields related to traditional Chinese culture, fields related to modern and contemporary Chinese culture
