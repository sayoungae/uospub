Dormitory

The purpose is to cultivate democratic order, a sense of solidarity, and leadership, creating a space for diverse interactions among seniors and juniors as a training ground for community living. It aims to enhance scholarly exchange programs and facilitate academic and student exchanges with prestigious universities abroad, shorten commuting time, provide conveniences for student learning, and foster disciplined and orderly communal living, thereby establishing an educational environment that promotes character development.
