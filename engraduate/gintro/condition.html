General Status of Graduate School

The graduate school aims to cultivate talents equipped with profound academic theories and professional knowledge based on fundamental knowledge. The graduate school offers 36 master's degree programs and 34 doctorate's degree programs in general courses, with master's programs established in Environmental Engineering and Chemical Engineering through cooperative programs with the Korea Institute of Science and Technology, the National Institute of Forest Science, the Seoul Health and Environment Research Institute, and the Korea Energy Technology Institute Additionally, interdepartmental cooperative programs include master's and doctorate's degree programs in Smart City and Urban Big Data Convergence studies, as well as a doctorate's degree program in International Urban Development and Disaster Science.
