Defining Global Excellence With Seoul

Welcome to the University of Seoul Graduate School
Thank you for your keen interest and warm support.



The University of Seoul (UOS) was founded in 1918, and the UOS Graduate School was established in 1980. Despite its short history, it has produced over 5,000 master's and doctoral graduates contributing to various fields as an international-level graduate school for top-tier education and research in Korea.


Our graduate school consists of about 400 world-class professors leading various research fields, and we are conducting various domestic and international research projects with cutting-edge facilities and equipment.


In particular, we are leaping to the world together with Seoul, leading the rapidly changing digital world by converging advanced fields such as intelligent semiconductors, artificial intelligence, big data, smart cities with existing engineering, natural sciences, humanities, social sciences, arts and physical education, and various interdisciplinary programs.


We are innovating the curriculum through the graduate school innovation support project to reflect various social demands and requirements, and providing programs such as academic research.
We are also pursuing international exchanges and cooperation in line with the global era.


The future we encounter is no longer a subject of prediction.
We will help you become the 'only one' with the ability to create your own future at our graduate school.


We look forward to your interest and participation in growing and developing together.


Dean Song Oseong
