A Place Where Recklessness is a Pride,
Graduate School of the University of Seoul

In today’s world, where we are accustomed to converting everything into practical value in reality, the act of pursuing graduate studies out of a desire to know may seem like a reckless choice when viewed through the lens of practicality. What awaits in graduate school is the repeated failure, frustration, self-doubt, and anxiety about the future that will arise on the journey of seeking and creating knowledge on your own.



However, the Graduate School of the University of Seoul is a place where such recklessness, which sets aside practical considerations and confronts the hardships and doubts head-on, converges. It is a place where this recklessness transforms reality into the future.



The Graduate School of the University of Seoul is a place where the belief that the future cannot be created through realistic calculations is shared. It is a place where the mindset that "failure is inevitable and frustration exists to be overcome" is embraced. Therefore, the Graduate School of the University of Seoul will be a place where, when we look back on the time we spent here in the future, our hearts will swell with the preciousness of those moments.



Here, we encourage each other.
Here, we help each other.
Here, we share in the failures and frustrations, and ultimately, we overcome them together.
That is why here, "recklessness becomes a pride."



Thank you.
