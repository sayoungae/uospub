Office of International Affairs - Special Admission For International Students
The University of Seoul’s Office of International Affairs is responsible for the admission of international students and supports your growth as global leaders through various international programs. Global thinking and experience are essential To become the future leaders who will drive the era of globalization.

To achieve this, the Office of International Affairs provides opportunities to gain diverse experiences and knowledge abroad through exchange student, dual-degree, language training, and global internship programs, based on strengthening cooperation through agreements with prestigious universities around the world.

Cultivate your dreams, hopes, and abilities toward the world at the University of Seoul.
