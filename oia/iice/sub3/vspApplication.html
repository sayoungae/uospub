Study at UOS

Eligibility & Application

Eligibility & Requirements
  • Must have completed at least two semesters at home university when they participate the program
  • A minimum GPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale (GPA by US standard) or grades equivalent to a B average (over 80% out of 100%)
  • Must be a currently enrolled student from one of UOS Partner Universities (please check the list of UOS partner universities first)
  • Must pay the tuition fee to the University of Seoul
  • No language requirement. But applicant should have good command of wither Korean or English.
Application Process

Step 1 : Submit online application (Click the button below)

신청 | Application

Step 2 : Submit required documents to exchangein@uos.ac.kr by the deadline

  • (1) Copy of Passport
  • (2) Photo
  • (3) Certificate of Transcript
  • (4) High School Diploma
  • (5) Official Certificate of Enrollment from the applicant’s home university
  • (6) Recent (within 30 days) Bank Statement (PDF) in your name or your parent’s name(at least 6,000,000KRW for 1 semester, 14,000,000KRW for 2 semesters)
  • (7) Certificate of Family Relations (only If you have a bank statement in your parent’s name)

  • ※ Documents No. 4 and No. 5 should be apostille or notarized from the Embassy of South Korea. This is mandatory for non-OECD nationalities

    * OECD Countries: Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Chile, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Germany, Japan, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Mexico, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, UK, USA.

    ※ Chinese students must confirm documents with the China Academic Degree (CHSI)

Application Deadline
  • Fall Admission : May 31
  • Spring Admission : November 30
