Korean Studies

In the morning from 9 to 12 on each class-day, there will be 8 special lectures on the various aspects of Korea.

  • (1) K Mass-Media
  • (2) K Webtoons
  • (3) K Drama & Movie
  • (4) K Pop
  • (5) K Café, Food and Drink Culture
  • (6) K UNESCO World Heritage
  • (7) K Business
  • (8) K Economics

    * Above themes are subject to change.

Korean Language

According to the level of the students, UOS SISSⅡ will open classes of Korean Language. Students are asked to indicate their level of Korean Language upon their application. Korean Language classes will be in the afternoon from 1 to 4 on each class-day.
