About the Program

Why Choose University of Seoul?
  • “Study at the heart of Seoul”
    Located in the vibrant capital city, the University of Seoul (UOS) is renowned as one of Korea’s top public universities. Since its founding in 1918, UOS has played a key role in shaping Korean society by educating and training students while serving as a vital Think Tank for the Seoul Metropolitan Government. UOS offers a broad range of academic programs across 8 Colleges and 9 Graduate Schools, providing diverse opportunities for learning.
  • About Seoul Visiting Student Program (SVSP)
    The Seoul Visiting Student Program(SVSP) offers students from partner universities a unique chance to study in Korea for up to one year. If you were not nominated as an exchange student, you can still apply to join UOS as a visiting student. As a member of UOS, you will be able to take advantage of our academic resources and experience life in Seoul.
  • Course Information
    • Credits: Visiting students can enroll in up to 20 credits per regular semester (spring or fall).
    • Courses: Most courses are taught in Korean. You can find detailed course information at: UOS Website → Academics → Each Department → Courses
    • English-Taught Courses: A selection of courses taught in English is also available for international students. Visit the List of Courses Offered in English for more details.
  • Accommodation
    Please note that on-campus dormitory accommodation at the University of Seoul is not guaranteed for visiting students. However, we provide comprehensive guidance and resources to help students find suitable off-campus housing. For more detailed information and assistance, please visit the Off-Campus Housing Information page.
