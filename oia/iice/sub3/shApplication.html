Study at UOS

Eligibility & Application

Eligibility

Student must be enrolled at one of our partner institutions as an undergraduate or graduate students for the duration of the program. Applicants must have a passport valid for at least 6 months

Applying Process

Nomination(Partner Univ) → Application(By Students) → Payment(Invoice to Students) → Admission → Study at SISS Ⅰ

  • Step 1

    Nomination (from February 1st to April 30th, 2025)

    The International Offices of UOS partner universities are asked to nominate candidate(s) at http://oia.uos.ac.kr
    Reference : UOS OIA Website → For International Students → Studying in UOS → Seoul International Summer SchoolⅠ → Nomination

  • Step 2

    Application (from February 1st to May15th, 2025)

    The International Offices of UOS partner universities are asked to nominate candidate(s) at http://oia.uos.ac.kr
    Reference : UOS OIA Website → For International Students → Studying in UOS → Seoul International Summer SchoolⅠ → Nomination

  • Step 3

    Payment (from March 1st to May15th, 2025)

    Payment information will be provided to Applicants during application period.(wire transfer)
    Payment period is from March 1st to May 15th, 2025

  • Step 4

    Admission (By May30th, 2025)

    Upon completion of the review of application and payment, UOS uploads the asmission packages (soft copy) on the page of CHECK STATUS on Eligility & Application. Applicants can download by onself. We can send the hard copy only upon request.
