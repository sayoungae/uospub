Housing
On-campus housing will be guaranteed and housing fees are supported by UOS. Detailed information on the facilities of the UOS dormitory can be found at http://www.uos.ac.kr/en -> Campus life -> Living at UOS

< On-campus Dormitory: International House, 1 Student per 1 Room >

Housing Assignment

On-campus housing is assigned according to gender. Students will be allocated to dormitories based on this gender division, and further instructions will be provided accordingly.

For Gender Minorities

Due to the current housing allocation policy, students who identify as non-binary or third-gender are required to seek off-campus housing. Please note that specific assistance with finding off-campus housing is not provided.
