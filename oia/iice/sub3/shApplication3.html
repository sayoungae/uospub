Eligibility & Application

자격 ㅣ Eligibility

학생은 프로그램 기간 동안 학부 또는 대학원 학생으로 파트너 기관 중 한 곳에 등록되어 있어야 합니다. 신청자는 최소 6개월 동안 유효한 여권을 소지해야 합니다.

Student must be enrolled at one of our partner institutions as an undergraduate or graduate students for the duration of the program. Applicants must have a passport valid for at least 6 months
신청절차 ㅣ Applying Process

추천(파트너 대학) → 신청(학생 본인) → 납부(학생에게 청구서) → Admission → SIWS 에서 공부

Nomination(Partner Univ) → Application(By Students) → Payment(Invoice to Students) → Admission → Study at SIWS

  • Step 1

    Nomination (from October 1st to November 30th, 2024)

    The International offices of UOS partner universities are asked to nominate candidate(s) at http://oia.uos.ac.kr
    - Reference: UOS OIA Website ▶ For International Students ▶ Studying in UOS ▶ Seoul International Winter School ▶ Nomination.

  • Step 2

    Application (from October1st to December 10th, 2024)

    Nominated students are required to complete the UOS on-line application at http://oia.uos.ac.kr
    - Reference: UOS OIA Website ▶ For International Students ▶ Studying in UOS ▶ Seoul International Winter School ▶ Eligibility & Application

  • Step 3

    Payment (from October 1st to December 10th, 2024)

    Payment can be made right afer completing Application. Detailed information about Payment can be found athttp://oia.uos.ac.kr
    - Reference: UOS OIA Website ▶ For International Students ▶ Studying in UOS ▶ Seoul International Winter School ▶ Payment

  • Step 4

    Admission (by December 20th, 2024)

    Upon completion of the review of application and payment, UOS uploads the admission packages (soft copy) on the webpage of CHECK STATUS on Eligibility & Application. Applicants can download it by oneself. We can send the hard copy only upon request.
