Track 1

#BusinessinKoreanPerspective #KoreanEconomy #AsianMarket #GlobalBusiness

he "Business in Korean perspective" track offers students a chance to learn about the Korean economy, business practices, and global marketing management in the context of the Asian market. Through lectures and hands-on experiences, students will gain a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities facing Korean and other Asian businesses in the global marketplace, and explore a special topic in global business.

Track 2

#KoreanCultureStudies #KoreanStudies #KoreanPopCulture #KoreanHistory

The "Korean Culture Studies" track at the University of Seoul Summer School program offers lectures and hands-on experiences to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of Korean culture and society, with a special focus on mass media and popular culture. The program offers a range of special topics in Korean studies, such as Korean Tradition and Culture, Korean History, and Mass Media and Popular culture in Korea
