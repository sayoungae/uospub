Tuition Refund Policy

In any of the following cases, tuition fees paid shall be refunded.

  • Where admission (including re-admission and transferring studies) or study cannot be continued pursuant to laws or school regulations
  • Where a person who has been granted admission has expressed his/her intention to give up admission
  • Where a student has indicated his/her intention to drop out
  • Where he/she has been prevented from entering the school due to his/her disease, death, natural disaster, or other unavoidable reasons, or has been prevented from continuing his/her studies

The amount to be refunded pursuant to the provisions of the preceding paragraph shall be classified as follows.

Date of occurrence of reason for return Return amount
Until the day before the entrance date or the day before the start date of the relevant semester The full amount of tuition
From the start of the semester to the 30th day 5/6 of tuition
From 31st day after the start of the semester to the 60th day 2/3 of the tuition
From the 61st day after the start of the semester to the 90th day Half of the tuition
90 days after the start of the semester No return
※ Notice Regarding Refunds to Overseas Accounts

For tuition fee refunds processed to overseas accounts, all bank fees incurred will be borne by the recipient. Please note that the refund amount will be disbursed after deducting the applicable fees.
