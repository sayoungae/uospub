Seoul Global Ambassador (SGA)
The UOS Office of International Affairs selects students among enrolled students who have pride as university members and trains them as Seoul Global Ambassadors (SGA) through education. SGA students serve as the face of the UOS Office of International Affairs, handling foreign guest protocol, and help international students adapt to school life at UOS.
Information

Eligibility

  • Undergraduate students of UOS who are expected to be enrolled
  • Those with excellent English conversation skills (direct response to overseas delegations)
  • Those who are interested in international exchange and can actively participate with school loyalty, sincerity, and responsibility
  • Those who have no disqualifications according to scholarship payment regulations

Activities

  • Official protocol for visiting delegations from overseas partner universities
    (directly introducing and promoting the school, and conducting campus tours)
  • Assisting with orientation for visiting and exchange international students and helping with school life adaptation through campus tours
  • Running and managing the social media accounts of the UOS Office of International Affairs

Application (in February)

  • Selection process: document submission → document and interview screening → selection of successful candidates
  • Selection criteria: selected considering motivation, activity plan, conversation skills, etc.

Benefits

  • Additional points given when applying for exchange student programs
  • Payment of work-study (scholarship) by summing up time in activity report
  • Group uniform (formal attire)
