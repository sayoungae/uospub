About Us

The University of Seoul, a leading institution of higher education, has been shaping the future of our community for over 100 years. Established in 1918, UOS is widely regarded as one of the top public universities in Korea. It has served as a think tank for the Seoul Metropolitan Government, contributing to the development of Korean society through education, student training, and academic research. With 8 colleges and 10 graduate schools, UOS offers a diverse range of courses in a thriving academic environment.
At the Office of International Affairs, we play a central role in developing and promoting various overseas exchange programs at UOS. Guided by our school's motto of "Truth, Creation, and Service," we are dedicated to cultivating talents essential for Korean society and the world.
Each year, we host nearly 2,000 international students who participate in our degree programs, Student Exchange Program, Seoul International Summer Schools, and Korean language and culture programs. We offer a wide range of opportunities for students to immerse themselves in Korean culture and academic life.
We extend a warm welcome to international students and faculty seeking a new life in Korea and Seoul. We are here to support and guide you every step of the way. Come and be a part of our diverse and dynamic community at UOS!
