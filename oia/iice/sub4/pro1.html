Overview

  • To ease the scheduling or financial burden of participating in long-term study abroad programs, a variety of short-term programs are available during summer and winter vacations.

    ※ Students take regular courses provided by overseas partner universities during vacation and transfer the credits to UOS.

Credit transfer

  • Students take regular courses offered by overseas universities during vacation, transfer the credits as major, general or elective courses, and have them recognized after returning (up to 6 credits for seasonal semester credit transfers).
