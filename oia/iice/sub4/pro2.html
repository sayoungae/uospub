Criteria & Scholarships

Criteria

Eligible candidates

  • Undergraduate students who have completed at least two semesters at UOS before studying abroad

Ineligible candidates

  • Those who have a record of disciplinary action (excluding academic disciplinary warnings)
  • Students who have exceeded the maximum study period (undergraduate students registered for 9 semesters or more; 11 semesters or more for architecture majors)
  • Graduate students, students on leave of absence, foreign exchange students at UOS, and students expected to graduate in February 2025
  • UOS Office of International Affairs-sponsored short-term study abroad, and previous participants in overseas language study programs
  • Students planning to participate in other internal or external programs that overlap with the period of participation in this program
  • Students taking winter semester courses at UOS
Scholarships
  • Differential scholarships provided by region for students who complete prerequisites
