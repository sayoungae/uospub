Eligibility and methods

Eligibility

  • Must be currently enrolled or on leave of absence and have completed at least one semester (must complete at least two semesters before the study abroad date)
  • For students on leave of absence, they must be registered at UOS before the study abroad date
  • Must have a cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
  • Must possess foreign language proficiency sufficient to study in the relevant country

  • Must not have been subject to disciplinary action according to school regulations (excl. academic warnings)

    ※ Check the eligibility requirements of the SAF member university you are applying to.

    http://www.studyabroadfoundation.org/saf-pdf/EntryRequirementGuidelines.pdf

Criteria

  • First-round selection by the University of Seoul (based on academic performance, English proficiency, and academic plan)

  • Second-round final selection by the SAF member university


    ※ Students selected by our university can apply to up to three universities, with final selection by the host university
